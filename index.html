Our tours

This 5-day adventure takes you to Iceland, a land of dramatic landscapes and the perfect place to witness the mesmerizing Northern Lights. Explore glaciers, waterfalls, and geothermal pools, and embark on nightly excursions to hunt for the aurora borealis.

Highlights: Go on a Northern Lights hunt, explore the Golden Circle, visit the Jokulsarlon glacier lagoon, soak in the Blue Lagoon geothermal spa, and experience the unique Icelandic culture.

Duration: 5 days Travel style: Adventure, nature lover Ideal for: Travelers seeking a unique and unforgettable experience in search of the Northern Lights

Embark on a 12-day exploration of the historic Silk Road, traveling through Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, and Tajikistan. Immerse yourself in the region's fascinating history, marvel at its architectural wonders, and experience the warm hospitality of its people

Highlights: Explore the ancient cities of Samarkand and Bukhara, visit the Tian Shan Mountains, trek through the Ala Archa National Park, learn about the traditional crafts and customs of the region, and enjoy delicious local cuisine.

Duration: 12 days Travel style: Cultural immersion, history buff Ideal for: Travelers interested in history, culture, and adventure

This 7-day expedition takes you to the Galapagos Islands, a UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its unique wildlife and diverse ecosystems. Hike through volcanic landscapes, observe giant tortoises and marine iguanas in their natural habitat, and learn about the groundbreaking research that took place on these islands

Highlights: Visit different islands with unique ecosystems, see a variety of endemic animals, go snorkeling and diving in crystal-clear waters, learn about the work of Charles Darwin, and contribute to conservation efforts.

Duration: 7 days Travel style: Wildlife encounter, nature lover Ideal for: Travelers interested in nature, wildlife, and conservation

This 10-day adventure takes you on a captivating journey through Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, and Thailand, exploring the vibrant cultures, stunning landscapes, and ancient temples that line the mighty Mekong River.

Cruise down the Mekong River, visit the Angkor Wat temple complex, explore the floating markets of Siem Reap, trek through the lush jungles of Laos, and learn about the rich history and traditions of the region.

Duration: 10 days Travel style: Adventure, cultural immersion Ideal for: Travelers seeking a unique and immersive experience in Southeast Asia
